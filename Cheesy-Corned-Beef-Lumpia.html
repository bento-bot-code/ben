Cheesy Corned Beef Lumpia Recipe

A savory twist on the classic lumpia, perfect for merienda or party snacks.

Ingredients:

1 can corned beef
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 small carrot, grated (optional)
1 cup grated cheese (quickmelt or cheddar)
Lumpia wrappers
Cooking oil for frying

Instructions:

1. Prepare the filling: Mix corned beef, onion, carrot, and cheese in a bowl.
2. Wrap the lumpia: Place a spoonful of the mixture on a lumpia wrapper. Roll tightly and seal the edge with water.
3. Fry until golden: Heat oil in a pan and fry the lumpia until crispy and golden brown.
4. Serve hot: Pair with ketchup, sweet chili sauce, or vinegar with garlic.
