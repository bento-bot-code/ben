Filipino-Style Taco Salad

Ingredients:

250g ground beef
2 tbsp canola oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 onion, finely chopped
1 tsp cumin powder
Salt and pepper, to taste
1/2 kilo lettuce leaves, chopped
2 tomatoes, sliced
1/4 cup cheddar cheese, grated
1 cup tortilla chips, crushed
3 tbsp cilantro (wansoy), chopped
1/2 cup sour cream

Filipino Dressing:

2 tbsp cane vinegar
1/4 cup canola oil
3 tbsp tomato sauce
1/2 tbsp sugar
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Saut√© garlic and onion in oil until translucent. Add ground beef, cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook until browned and set aside.
2. Blend all dressing ingredients until smooth. Adjust seasoning.
3. On a platter, layer lettuce, tomatoes, tortilla chips, cheese, and cilantro.
4. Add cooked beef and drizzle with sour cream and dressing. Toss gently and serve immediately.
Total time:25-30 mins Servings:4 portions
