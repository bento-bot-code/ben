Milo Pancake Recipe

Ingredients:

1 cup all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons Milo powder
2 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 egg
¾ cup milk or water
1 tablespoon melted butter or oil

Instructions:

1. Mix dry ingredients: In a bowl, combine flour, Milo, sugar, and baking powder.
2. Add wet ingredients: Stir in egg, milk (or water), and melted butter until smooth.
3. Cook pancakes: Heat a nonstick pan over medium heat. Pour ¼ cup batter per pancake. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook the other side until browned.
4. Serve: Enjoy plain or with condensed milk, peanut butter, or sliced bananas.
Total time:20 mins
Servings: 4-6 medium size pancakes
